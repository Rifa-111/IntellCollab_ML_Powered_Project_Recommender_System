ResearchGate

ResearchGate is a professional network for scientists and researchers to share and access scientific publications, research projects, and collaborate with peers globally.

Visit ResearchGate

IEEE Xplore

IEEE Xplore provides access to scientific and technical content published by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) and its publishing partners, including journals, conference proceedings, and standards.

Visit IEEE Xplore

Google Scholar

Google Scholar is a freely accessible web search engine that indexes the full text or metadata of scholarly literature across an array of publishing formats and disciplines.

Visit Google Scholar

PubMed

PubMed is a free search engine accessing primarily the MEDLINE database of references and abstracts on life sciences and biomedical topics. The United States National Library of Medicine (NLM) at the National Institutes of Health maintains the database as part of the Entrez system of information retrieval.
